1. 段落 <p>:

源代码:
    <p>This is a paragraph<p>
    <p>This is anotherparagraph<p>

This is a paragraph

This is another paragraph

  1. 浏览器会自动在块级元素前后添加空行;
  2. 不要使用两个<p>来代替一个换行!


2. 换行 <br />:

源代码:
    <p>This is a paragraph <br />
    This is another paragraph.
    </p>

This is a paragraph
This is another paragraph.
